Presentations

Presentations is an ideal course for students at intermediate level or above who want to develop their business communication skills for presentations in English.

The video course takes students step-by-step through the essential skills and language needed to give successful presentations in English. A series of tasks are available to put the skills and language learnt into practice. These tasks can be completed alone or by practising with other students online for free.

Students can also practice their presentations with a qualified tutor to get expert feedback.
